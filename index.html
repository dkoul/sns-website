The Art of Sozni

Centuries of Heritage in Every Stitch

Sozni is a traditional form of Kashmiri embroidery, known for its intricate needlework and delicate patterns. Each piece can take months to complete, with artisans dedicating their lives to perfecting this ancient craft.

Our collection honors these master craftspeople, preserving techniques that have been passed down through generations while creating timeless pieces for discerning collectors.
