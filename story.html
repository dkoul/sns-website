The Vision

Sheen & Sozni was born from a deep reverence for Kashmiri artistry and a desire to preserve centuries-old embroidery traditions in a modern world.

Founded by Bhawana, our atelier is more than a luxury brand—it's a bridge between generations of master artisans and collectors who appreciate true craftsmanship.

Each piece tells a story: of hands that have perfected their craft over decades, of patterns passed down through families, and of a heritage we are honored to share with the world.

"Every stitch is a meditation. Every piece is a labor of love. We don't create fashion—we preserve heritage."

— Bhawana, Founder

Our Philosophy

We believe in slow luxury—the antithesis of fast fashion. Our pieces can take months to complete, with artisans dedicating countless hours to perfecting every detail.

This is not mass production. This is art. This is heritage. This is the future of sustainable luxury.
